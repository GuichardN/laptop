About Me

Hello, my name is Guichard. From gaming to skateboarding to modifying cars, I’ve always had an itch to create. I started out making edits — syncing clips to music and cutting everything together so it flowed. That same energy carried into cars. I picked up my first camera to shoot my own builds, and once I got comfortable, I started shooting my friends’ cars too. This site is just a place to showcase some of those shoots — the moments we’ve captured along the way. I still shoot with the same approach: keep it clean, keep it flowing, keep it real. And honestly, time always flies when I’m out there shooting
